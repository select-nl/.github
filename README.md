# Select.nl

**Select** is a Dutch IT staffing and recruitment company dedicated to connecting talented IT professionals with ambitious organisations across the Netherlands.

## What We Do

At Select.nl, we believe the right people make all the difference. We specialise in matching skilled IT professionals — from developers and architects to data engineers and security specialists — with companies that are shaping the future through technology.

Whether you are an IT professional looking for your next challenge or an organisation searching for the perfect candidate, Select.nl offers:

- **Recruitment & Search** — Permanent placement of IT specialists tailored to your organisation's culture and technical requirements.
- **Contracting** — Flexible deployment of experienced IT freelancers and contractors for short- or long-term projects.
- **Consultancy** — Expert advice on workforce planning, IT talent strategy, and market insights in the Dutch technology sector.

## Our Approach

We combine deep sector knowledge with a personal approach. Our consultants speak the language of technology and take the time to understand both the candidate's ambitions and the client's goals — ensuring long-lasting, successful matches.
